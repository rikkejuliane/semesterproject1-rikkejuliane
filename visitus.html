ADMISSION

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOKper person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

ACCESSIBILITY

The museum is well-equipped for accessibility, featuring wheelchair ramps for easy access, audio guides for an immersive experience, and braille signs for visitors with visual impairments, ensuring everyone can enjoy the exhibits.

FOOD AND DRINK

The museum's attached café offers light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, and snacks, providing a pleasant spot for visitors to grab a bite or refreshment during their visit.

SHOP

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
