EXPLORE TOGETHER

Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we’re committed to making science accessible to all.

EXPLORE

Prepared for a journey of discovery?

Over the course of human history, science has developed from our early understanding of fire, wind, water, and earth to exploring everything from galaxies far away to the very building blocks of life itself.

DO YOU HAVE AN INNER EXPLORER?

The aim of our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continue the exploration of the world around us.

We believe in making science come alive through interactive exhibits. Please note that our dinosaur exhibits only come to life after hours.

EXPLORE

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? Come on down to our museum, there’s plenty to see and do.

You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth? Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore.

We also have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts.

During the school holidays we run special holiday clubs where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. To find out more about the holiday club and how you can join, send us a message .

Are you an educator in search of enriching science resources for your students? Our museum offers a comprehensive educational experience, brimming with engaging exhibits and hands-on learning opportunities designed to spark curiosity and foster discovery.

As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create places of learning and exploring. Our team are on hand to give your students guided tours of the museum, teach them in our learning laboratory, and provide great video presentations that will excite and inspire them. Our goal is to make science enjoyable for all students, helping to ignite a passion for discovery.

Please get in touch with us here to find out more about our facilities and to arrange a time to bring your students through for a visit. Let's work together to create an unforgettable learning experience that enriches the minds of the next generation.

Are you a researcher looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics?

By joining us, you will have the opportunity to delve into unique research projects, utilize our specialized facilities, and work alongside a network of professionals dedicated to advancing knowledge in our field. Whether your interest lies in historical data analysis, experimental research, or interdisciplinary studies, our platform is designed to support and amplify your work.

We encourage you to get in touch with us here to explore how you can make the most of our resources and contribute to our collective research goals. Your expertise, experience and insights could play a vital role in enhancing our understanding of the subjects we're passionate about. Let’s collaborate to push the boundaries of what we know and discover new perspectives together.

SPECIAL UPCOMING EVENTS

Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.

Night in the Museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

Energetica Exhibition

The Energetica exhibition, on loan from Amsterdam's NEMO Science Museum, arrives at the Community Science Museum, featuring interactive displays on harnessing elemental power, including solar lighting and wind turbines.
